## Content Strategy

### Content Types

| Format | Frequency | Length | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Shorts** | 2-3x/week | 30-60s | Quick tips, hot takes, clips |
| **Tutorials** | 2x/month | 10-20min | How-to deploy, configure, build |
| **Build in Public** | 2x/month | 15-30min | Longer dev sessions, architecture talks |
| **Industry Analysis** | 1x/month | 10-15min | Market trends, technology comparisons |

### Video Style
- Screen share + face cam (picture-in-picture)
- Pop-art branded intro bumper (3-5 seconds)
- Lower thirds in brand colors (Magenta/Cyan)
- End screen with subscribe CTA
- Thumbnail style: Bold text, brand colors, face reaction

### Thumbnail Template
- Background: Brand gradient or solid color
- Large text: 3-5 words max, Instrument Sans Bold
- Your face or a reaction expression
- Brand element: halftone dot texture or star accent
- High contrast, readable at small sizes

### YouTube-Specific Tips
- Optimize titles for search (keyword research)
- Write detailed descriptions with timestamps
- Use cards and end screens for cross-linking
- Create playlists by topic
- Pin a "start here" comment on every video
- Respond to comments in the first 24 hours
