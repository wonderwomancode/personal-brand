## Content Strategy

### Content Mix
- 35% Technical discussion and open source commentary
- 25% Build in public
- 20% Boosting (retweeting) quality content from others
- 20% Community engagement and replies

### Mastodon-Specific Norms
- **CW (Content Warning)** tags: Use for potentially sensitive topics, long threads, or controversial takes
- **Alt text**: ALWAYS add alt text to images (community strongly values accessibility)
- **No quote-toots** (historically): Mastodon culture prefers replies over quote-tweeting, though this is evolving
- **Anti-corporate tone**: Be genuine and human, not promotional
- **Hashtags matter**: Mastodon search relies heavily on hashtags (no full-text search on most instances)
- **Thread structure**: Use `1/n` format for threads
