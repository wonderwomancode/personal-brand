### SEO/AEO Optimization
- Title: Include target keyword
- Subtitle: Secondary keyword + value proposition
- First 100 words: Answer the core question directly
- Headers: Phrase as questions where natural
- Include comparison tables (AI engines love structured data)
- Use code blocks for technical content
- Add 5 relevant tags per article

### Publishing Schedule
- 1-2 articles per month (quality over quantity)
- Publish Tuesday or Wednesday morning (EST)
- Share to all social platforms within 1 hour of publishing
- Follow up with a Twitter/X thread summarizing key points

### Medium-Specific Tips
- Use the "series" feature for multi-part content
- Respond to every comment (Medium algorithm rewards engagement)
- Clap for and highlight other writers in your space
- Cross-post to Dev.to with canonical URL pointing to Medium
